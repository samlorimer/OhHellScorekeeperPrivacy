# Oh Hell Scorekeeper - Support

Thank you for using Oh Hell Scorekeeper! If you are experiencing issues, have questions, or would like to request a feature, we are here to help.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I start a new game?
From the main menu, tap **"New Game"**. You will be prompted to select players from your existing roster or add new ones. Once you have selected the players, you can arrange their seating order and choose the starting dealer.

### I made a mistake entering a score. Can I fix it?
Yes! To correct a past round:
1. Tap on the **Scorecard** for the active game.
2. Long-press (or swipe) on the round you need to correct.
3. Select **Edit** or **Correct Round**.
4. Adjust the bids and tricks as necessary. The app will automatically recalculate the scores for that round and all subsequent rounds.

### Where can I manage my players?
You can add, rename, or delete players by navigating to **Settings > Manage Players** from the main menu. Deleting a player will retain their historical game data but remove them from the active selection list.

### Why won't the app let me enter the final bid?
Oh Hell rules stipulate that the total number of bids in a round cannot equal the number of cards dealt. This ensures that at least one player will miss their bid (the "Hook" rule). The app enforces this rule automatically.
